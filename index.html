Our Story

Quiet Village,
Endless Blue

Tucked into the sun-drenched village of Ruskamen on the Omiš Riviera, Apartmani Sanja is a family-run retreat where the Adriatic begins at the foot of your terrace. Ancient pine trees shade your mornings. The smell of the sea is constant.

We offer four thoughtfully furnished apartments — from a cosy studio for two to a spacious family apartment with two bedrooms. Whether you're here for the Cetina River canyon, the quiet coves, or simply the sound of pebbles on the shore, we look forward to welcoming you.

The house is looked after personally by the Čulić family. Guests are our guests, not bookings.
