Our Place

A Quiet Corner
of Dalmatia

Tucked into the sun-warmed village of Ruskamen on the Omiš Riviera, Apartmani Sanja sits where ancient pines shade the stone path to the sea. Below the terrace, the Adriatic stretches south. Twenty metres down, a pebble beach.

We offer four thoughtfully furnished apartments — from an intimate studio for two to a generous family apartment with two bedrooms. Whether you've come for the Cetina River canyon, the quiet coves, or simply the sound of stones and waves, the Čulić family will welcome you personally.

"Our guests are our guests — not bookings."

Beach at Your Feet

A pristine pebble cove begins just 20 metres from your terrace. Clear Adriatic water, no crowds — just the sound of the sea.

Family-Run Warmth

Hosted personally by Sanja and Marin Čulić. No call centres, no agency fees — just honest hospitality and helpful local knowledge.

BBQ under the Pines

A shared courtyard grill area shaded by old pine trees. Summer evenings, fresh fish, and the smell of the sea — a Dalmatian ritual.

Riviera Panoramas

From terraces above the Adriatic, watch sunsets paint the islands gold. Omiš canyon 6 km east. Split and the open sea 30 km west.
